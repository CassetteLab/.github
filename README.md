# CassetteLab

Welcome to **CassetteLab**.

We build projects that are free and open-source, with a simple goal: create useful software for people.

## Our projects

You can explore our work in the repositories of the CassetteLab organization, where we share tools, experiments, and products designed to be accessible, transparent, and community-driven.

## Our vision

At CassetteLab, we believe software should empower people.
That is why we focus on:

- Free and open-source development
- Practical tools that solve real needs
- Collaboration with and for the community
