# Cassette Lab

**Open-source music software you actually own. Free forever, for everyone.**

Cassette Lab builds software for people who want their music library to be *theirs* — self-hosted, private, and free of the tricks that big-tech apps use to lock you in. No ads, no tracking, no subscriptions, no dark patterns. Just your music.

We believe the tools you use every day shouldn't hold your data hostage or rent you access to your own collection. So everything we make is open-source, self-hosted-first, and **100% free — today and forever.**

---

## What we stand for

- **Free for everyone, always.** No paywalls, no in-app purchases, no premium tier. Support is welcome but never required.
- **Open source by default.** Every line is public. Read it, fork it, improve it, run it yourself.
- **Self-hosted-first.** Your library lives on *your* server ([Navidrome](https://www.navidrome.org/), Subsonic, or any OpenSubsonic-compatible backend). We're a client, not a landlord.
- **Privacy is not a feature, it's the baseline.** No analytics harvesting, no accounts you don't control, credentials stay on your device.
- **Built to last.** Native, dependency-light, and boring in all the right ways.

## Projects

### 🎧 [Cassette](https://github.com/CassetteLab/cassette)
A native music app for iOS & macOS that streams your self-hosted library beautifully. Built in SwiftUI, powered by SwiftSonic, designed around one idea: *just your music.*

- Native iOS & macOS, single codebase
- Streams from Navidrome / Subsonic / OpenSubsonic servers
- AirPlay, scrobbling (ListenBrainz), ReplayGain, crossfade, offline downloads
- **License: MPL-2.0 · Free forever · No IAP, no subscriptions**

> 🤖 **An Android port is in the works** — [Cassette for Android](https://github.com/CassetteLab/cassette-android) — the ecosystem is opening up. More to come.

### 📦 [SwiftSonic](https://github.com/CassetteLab/swiftsonic)
A clean, modern Swift client library for the Subsonic & OpenSubsonic APIs. The engine under Cassette, usable in any Swift project.

- Swift 6, strict concurrency, zero external dependencies
- iOS · macOS · tvOS · watchOS · visionOS
- Typed errors, retry policies, security-first credential handling
- Indexed on the [Swift Package Index](https://swiftpackageindex.com/MathieuDubart/SwiftSonic)
- **License: MIT · Open source**

### 💿 [CrateDigger](https://github.com/CassetteLab/crate-digger)
A Swift-native client to communicate with your deemix instance.

- Swift 6, strict concurrency, zero external dependencies
- iOS · macOS · tvOS · watchOS · visionOS
- Typed errors, retry policies, security-first credential handling
- **License: MIT · Open source**
